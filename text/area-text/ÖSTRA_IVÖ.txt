Östra Ivö is a steep rocky hillside on the island of Ivö in the lake Ivösjön. The climbing here is wild and spread out, with varied rock types and problems in many different styles. Approaches are long, tough, and sometimes confusing, with uneven terrain and landings throughout.

This area won’t appeal to everyone — but for those willing to trade convenience and volume for solitude and quality, the reward is a collection of stoic, secluded lines deep in a quiet, mysterious forest. Here in particular, it pays to look for lines — not just numbers...
